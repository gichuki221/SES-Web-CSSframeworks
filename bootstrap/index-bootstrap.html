CATS

Siamese Cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • Distinctive color points on the ears, face, paws, and tail
  • Blue almond-shaped eyes
  • Short coat with a sleek appearance

The Siamese cat is known for its vocal nature and social personality.

American Curl

American Curl cats are intelligent, playful, and curious. Their ears curl towards the back of their head, and they form strong attachments to their owners.

Calico

Calico cats are known for being the world's most colorful cats. They are sweet, loving, and loyal.
